= On-Hold and Inactive Windows

You can configure windows (periods of time) during which partners or transactions are on-hold or inactive.

include::/_sources/edit-settings.adoc[]
[start=3]

. In the *CONFIGURATION* section of the left-hand navigation pane on the <<partner-configuration.adoc#img-company-information, Company Information Page>>, click *On-Hold/Inactive*.
+
The <<img-on-hold-inactive>> appears.

[[img-on-hold-inactive]]

image::on-hold-inactive.png[img-on-hold-inactive, title="On-Hold/Inactive Page"]


== To Add a New Partner Status Window

. On the <<img-on-hold-inactive>>, in the *Partner Status Windows* section, click *Create New Window*. +
The <<img-partner-status-window-add>> appears.

[[img-partner-status-window-add]]

image::partner-status-window-add.png[img-partner-status-window-add, title="Partner Status Window Page"]

[start=2]

. On the <<img-partner-status-window-add>>, in the *Type* box, click the down-arrow to expose the drop-down list.
. From the drop-down list, to create an on-hold window, click *ON-HOLD*. +
To create an inactive window, click *INACTIVE*.
. Use the calendars and dropdown lists in the Time Bar to identify the start and end dates and times for the window.
. Click *Save* to save the window.


== To Add a New Transaction Status Window

. On the <<img-on-hold-inactive>>, in the *Transaction Status Windows* section, click *Create New Window*. +
The <<img-transaction-status-window-add>> appears.

[[img-transaction-status-window-add]]

image::transaction-status-window-add.png[img-transaction-status-window-add, title="Transaction Status Window Page"]

[start=2]

. On the <<img-transaction-status-window-add>>, in the *Type* box, click the down-arrow to expose the drop-down list.
. From the drop-down list, to create an on-hold window, click *ON-HOLD*. + To create an inactive window, click *INACTIVE*.
. Use the calendars and dropdown lists in the Time Bar to identify the start and end dates and times for the window.
. To add a channel, in the *Channel* section, click *Add Channel*. +
The Pick a Channel Window appears.
. Select a channel, then click *Use selected*.
. Click *Save* to save the window.

== To Edit an On-Hold or Inactive Window

. On the <<img-on-hold-inactive>>, in the section that corresponds to the type of window you want to edit, identify the window you wan to edit and click it. +
The  appropriate page appears.
. On the page, make changes as appropriate.
. Click *Save* to save your changes.
